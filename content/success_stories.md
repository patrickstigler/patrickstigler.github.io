### Success Story at Robert Bosch GmbH, Stuttgart

I identified a yearly inventory counting process in central purchasing that required eight people and three weeks to complete. By developing a script, I streamlined the process, enabling the same task to be accomplished with a single click in just four minutes, significantly increasing efficiency and saving valuable time and resources.


### Building a Makerspace from Scratch at Robert Bosch GmbH, Stuttgart

I spearheaded the creation of a Makerspace from the ground up, transforming an empty area into a hub of innovation and creativity. Focusing on educational projects, I secured a free 3D printer for the space, enhancing our prototyping capabilities. 

I established several collaborations with universities, particularly in AR and VR, which enriched our research and development initiatives. The Makerspace became the foundation for major events, including hackathons that I hosted, fostering a community of inventors and problem-solvers.

This effort not only provided valuable resources for internal projects but also positioned our organization as a leader in technological innovation and collaboration.
